What is ALC?

ALC is an organisation established in 1992, supporting students, professionals, housewives, and locals for learning English language from the grass-root levels. Having observed the English proficiency of learners which is utterly on below average level, ALC has prepared a specific system of teaching ELP (English Language Program) through an innovative learning methodology in which anybody can learn so quickly than their expectations.
